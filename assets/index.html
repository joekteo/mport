PrediktApp

PrediktApp is a real-time sports gambling site that uses in-game currency. Users can get pro tips, compete & interact with other gamblers, and most of all practice their gambling skills without poking a hole in their pockets! Built using Ruby on Rails, JSON, PostgreSQL, AngularJs, CoffeeScript, and Slim.



MusicHolic

MusicHolic identifies songs being played around you and suggests an alcoholic beverage based the song's musical traits. The front end of this app was built using the GraceNote iOS SDK and Node.js as the back-end with MongoDB as the persistence layer. MusicHolic features a minimal and clean design, making clever use of UIView Animations, CoreImage (GaussianBlur), and CoreAnimation (Gradients).



AbramHealth
